The KBiff Handbook

Kurt Granroth <granroth@kde.org>
Version 2.3.6 June 03, 1999

KBiff is a KDE aware mail notification utility. It supports MBOX (Unix style), Maildir (Qmail), MH, POP3, IMAP4, and NNTP mailboxes.
