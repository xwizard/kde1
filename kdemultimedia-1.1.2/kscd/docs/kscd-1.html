1. Introduction

Kscd is a fast, CDDB enabled cd player for the Unix platform. Kscd stands for "The KDE Project's small/simple CD player".

Special thanks to David White who wrote the KSCD help documention! Thanks David!

New: workman2cddb.pl Perl script provided in order to faciliate transition for users of workman.

I hope you will enjoy this cd-player,
