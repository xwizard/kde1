1. Introduction

Some time ago I had downloaded xusermgr written with XForms. This program has a great interface but on my 800x600 display it looked very ugly. Some text labels on the buttons were out of bounds. Of course I started to complain about it. My friend heard me and said : "Why are you complaining? If you don't like something make it better!" I thought that he was right and wrote this primitive user manager.
