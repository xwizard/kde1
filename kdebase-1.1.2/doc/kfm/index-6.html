6. Thanks

KFM would not be what it is without so many people sending me bug reports, writing patches and extensions and many good ideas. Thanks to you all. There are some persons which I want to mention in detail:

  • Martin Jones wrote most of the HTML widget and is doing the painful job of bug fixing it and catching up with Netscapes latest broken HTML features.
  • Fritz Elfert wrote the automatic mime type detection stuff at KDE-ONE in Arnsberg.
  • Stephan Kulow did the automake and autoconf stuff and introduced me to the mysteries of CVS and is my last help in questions of platform independence.
  • Stefan Westerfeld rewrote the kioslave for KFM II.
  • Claudia Sorg, my girlfriend, she had to face my bad moods whenever KFM crashed again and it crashed very often.
