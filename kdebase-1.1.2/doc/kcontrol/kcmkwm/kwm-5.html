5. Configuring Mouse Button Actions

This screen allows you to configure the actions of clicks of the three mouse buttons while the pointer is over a window.

The Titlebar and frame settings allow configuration of the action of mouse button clicks while the pointer is over the titlebar of either Active windows (with focus) or Inactive windows (without focus). Choose the possible actions from those in the list boxes.

The Inactive inner window settings configure the action of mouse clicks when the pointer is over the interior of an inactive window.

The final Inner window, titlebar and frame settings configure the action of clicking the mouse buttons while the Alt key is held down, when the pointer is over any part of a window, whether active or not.
