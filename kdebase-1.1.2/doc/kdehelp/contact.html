Mailing Lists 

KDE maintains several mailing lists. A mailing list archive can be found here.

  • kde - for general discussion 
  • kde-announce - announcements of new KDE applications 
  • kde-user - users help each other 
  • kde-devel - for developers. This request is for read only, to have write access you must email  Martin Konold  our mailing list maintainer. Please tell him on which part of the project you are intending to work on. 
  • kde-licensing - discussion of licensing issues 
  • kde-look - discussing "look'n'feel issues" 
To Subscribe: Click on one of the above links and type subscribe your-email-address in the subject of the message. Please write your real email address instead of the plain "your-email-address" words. Leave the body of the message empty. Do not include a signature or other stuff which might confuse the mail server which processes your request. 

To Unsubscribe: Click on one of the above links and type unsubscribe your-email-address in the subject of the message. Please replace  "your-email-address" with the email address you did use for the subscription. Leave the body of the message empty. Do not include a signature or other stuff which might confuse the mail server which processes your request. 

Notes: 
Please do not use HTML to send your request because it makes it difficult for our mail server to process your request. If the subscription address is identical to the address you are posting from you may ommit the address in the subject line. 

Contact addresses for developers can be found in the documentation of their individual applications.
