maudio - audio player for the KDE environment

This help page was last updated on 25.1.1998

This player plays WAV medias. It is a mediatool compliant media player and is used by kmedia ande the KDE games. It can only play PCM coded WAV files.

  • It uses /dev/dsp. You cannot configure him to use other devices currently.
  • It grabs /dev/dsp and releases it after a short period of idle time.

This version should play most WAV medias flawlessy. Please note that ADPCM WAVs are not supported at all. Please let me know if Wav playing with kmedia and the KDE games works for you or not. Mail your comments to Christian Esken.
