4. Configuring the KDE International Keyboard.

There are three configuration dialogs for the KDE International Keyboard.

  • The General settings dialog, for selecting the keyboard map, and hot keys. This also accesses the Advanced Options dialog.
  • The Style dialog for configuring the fonts and colors used by the International Keyboard keyboard-indicator button.
  • The Startup dialog for configuring how the International Keyboard is started, and the placement of the keyboard-indicator button.
